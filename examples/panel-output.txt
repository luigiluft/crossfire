│ ### 2. No idempotency — Stripe retries double-credit the account
│ **Priority: CRITICAL**

│ ### 3. No row-level concurrency control on the balance update
│ **Priority: HIGH**
│ **Confidence: 4**

│ 
│ The fix is a unique constraint on the processed-event id and `INSERT ... ON CONFLICT DO NOTHING` returning whether a row was inserted, only applying the credit if the insert succeeded — all in one transaction.
